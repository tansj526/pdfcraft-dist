About This Tool

Header & Footer adds customizable headers and footers to your PDF documents. Include page numbers, dates, document titles, or any custom text in the header or footer areas.

Position content on the left, center, or right of the header/footer. Use different content for odd and even pages if needed. Perfect for creating professional documents with consistent formatting.

All processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring your documents remain private.

How to Use

  1. 1

    Upload Your PDF

    Drag and drop your PDF file or click to select the document.

  2. 2

    Configure Header/Footer

    Enter text for header and footer areas. Add page numbers, dates, or custom text.

  3. 3

    Apply and Download

    Click Apply to add headers/footers and download your updated PDF.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I have different headers on odd and even pages?

Yes, you can configure different content for odd and even pages.

Can I include the current date?

Yes, you can insert dynamic date fields that show the current date.

Can I skip the header/footer on certain pages?

Yes, you can specify which pages should have headers/footers and which should be skipped.
